Privacy Policy

I Ching Oracle · Michael Schwarzchild · Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy describes how the I Ching Oracle application ("the app") handles information when you use it.

Information We Do Not Collect

The I Ching Oracle does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

The app does not collect your name, email address, location, or any other identifying information. It does not track your usage or behavior. It does not use analytics services. It does not serve advertisements. It does not connect to any server or external service during normal use. It does not store your questions or readings anywhere outside your device.

How the App Works

The I Ching Oracle operates entirely on your device. All hexagram casting, interpretation, and reading generation happens locally. No data is sent to any external server. The only network requests the app makes are to load fonts from Google Fonts when an internet connection is available; no personal information is transmitted in these requests.

Saved Readings

If you choose to save a reading using the "Save This Reading" feature, the reading is downloaded as a plain text file to your device. This file remains entirely under your control and is not transmitted anywhere.

Children's Privacy

The I Ching Oracle does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. As the app collects no data whatsoever, it presents no privacy risk to users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Given the nature of the app, such changes are unlikely.
